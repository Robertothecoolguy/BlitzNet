Product Safety

General Usage Warning

To ensure safe operation, do not expose the BlitzNet Modem and SIM Card to extreme temperatures (below -10°C or above 60°C) or immerse it in water, as this may cause malfunction or permanent damage. Avoid using the device near open flames or in highly humid environments, as this could lead to overheating or short-circuiting. Always use the provided power adapter to prevent electrical hazards.

SIM Card Handling Precautions

When inserting or removing the BlitzNet SIM Card, use only the provided SIM ejection tool or a non-metallic object to remove the card—sharp or metallic objects may scratch the SIM or modem, potentially disrupting connectivity. Keep the SIM Card away from magnetic fields, as this could corrupt stored data and impair global roaming functionality.

Radiation and Interference Advisory

The BlitzNet Modem emits low-level radiofrequency (RF) energy during operation. While compliant with international safety standards, prolonged direct contact with the device during active use is not recommended. Maintain a distance of at least 20 cm between the modem and your body to minimize exposure. Do not place the modem near sensitive medical equipment, such as pacemakers, as RF signals may cause interference.

Environmental and Disposal Guidelines

To protect the environment, do not dispose of the BlitzNet Modem or SIM Card in household waste. The device contains materials that may be hazardous if not recycled properly. Use designated electronic waste recycling facilities in your region. In the event of a damaged or swollen battery, avoid puncturing or incinerating the modem, as this could release toxic fumes.

Child Safety Notice

Keep the BlitzNet Modem and SIM Card out of reach of children. Small components, including the SIM Card and ejection tool, pose a choking hazard if swallowed. Additionally, excessive exposure to the device’s LED indicators may cause discomfort to young eyes. Supervise usage in households with children to prevent accidental misuse or injury.

Tampering

Avoid tampering with the BlitzNet Modem's and SIM Card’s firmware, as this could result in permanent deactivation or legal consequences in certain jurisdictions.
